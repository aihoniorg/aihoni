Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

aihoni (“we”, “us”) helps people in Nepal stay close to family and friends with chat, voice, video and group calls and a personal AI साथी — and helps small businesses too. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it. We keep it short and plain on purpose.

Information we collect

  • Account info — your name, email and profile photo when you sign in with Google.
  • Business info — details about your business you add, such as products, prices and location.
  • Messages & voice — chats, voice recordings and orders you create, so aihoni can help you.
  • Usage data — basic, anonymous information about how the app is used to keep it reliable.

How we use it

  • To answer your customers and run features like voice chat, the smart inbox and your knowledge base.
  • To process orders and payments through your chosen wallet (eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay, ConnectIPS).
  • To improve aihoni’s accuracy and reliability.

What we don’t do

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your business data with other shops.

Your choices

You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account at any time by emailing hello@aihoni.com.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at hello@aihoni.com.
